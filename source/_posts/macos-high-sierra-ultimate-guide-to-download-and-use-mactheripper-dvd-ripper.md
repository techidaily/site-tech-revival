Such situation shall be boiled down into one reason. As we know, MacTheRipper official site has been shut down, bringing about inexistence of support team and unavailable software updates. So the free Mac dvd ripper requires Rosetta seems to be at a loss in the matter of Mac Lion operating system that dropped Rosetta support, coming on the heels of failure to rip DVD on Mac Lion with it.
