**What is 3GP???**

**3GP** (3GPP file format) is a multimedia container format defined by the Third Generation Partnership Project (3GPP) for 3G UMTS multimedia services. It is a common video and audio format for mobile phones, such as Nokia, Motorola, Sony Ericsson, Qualcomm and more.

If you want to view videos either from the internet or your computer on your 3GP mobile phones, you are supposed to **convert video to 3GP video format first**. This article will show you how to transcode MP4 to 3GP and the same method applies to decode AVI to 3GP, MKV to 3GP, YouTube to 3GP, etc. with the all-inclusive **MacX Video Converter Pro**.
