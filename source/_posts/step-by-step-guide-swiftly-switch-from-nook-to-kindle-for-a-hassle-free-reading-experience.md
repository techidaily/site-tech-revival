###  Step 1\. Download Nook Books to Computer

The Barnes & Noble eBooks are available in EPUB or PDF formats, while the Amazon Kindle only supports MOBI, AZW3, or KFX formats. That's why we need to convert Nook books to Kindle format. Just like many other eBook vendors, Barnes & Noble also employs a very strict DRM scheme to encrypt their Nook books. Before converting Nook ebooks to a Kindle-supported format, we have to remove drm from Nook books.

To remove Nook DRM, the first step is to download the Nook books to your computer. Barnes & Noble ebooks cannot be downloaded via web browsers anymore. Furthermore, the Nook desktop application has been removed from the Nook server for quite some time, making it difficult for Nook users to download their books. However, with some effort and research, it is still possible to download Nook ebooks to a computer.

### FAQs on converting Nook to Kindle Paperwhite

![](http://www.epubor.com/images/faq.png) Frequently Asked Questions

##### Q: Can I convert Nook to Kindle with Calibre?

A: Sorry, you can't convert Nook books to Kindle if you're unable to download your Nook books with the Nook for Windows app, which was discontinued last year. But you can use the [Epubor Nook Converter](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/nook-converter/) to convert Nook books to kindle format.

##### Q: How to download my Nook books to computer?

A: There are different ways to download Nook books. You can download nook books via Nook for windows App. But if you don't download nook for Windows app, you cannot use this method, but you can [download Nook books via nook for Android](https://tools.techidaily.com/epubor/products/). 

##### Q: Can I send Nook epub to kindle?

A: Yes, you can send Nook EPUB files to a Kindle Paperwhite. However, you'll need to remove the DRM from Nook books first because the 'Send to Kindle' feature only supports DRM-free EPUB files. 

##### Q: Can I transfer my Nook books to another device?

A: Yes, you can transfer your Nook books to another Nook device by syncing your Nook account. When transferring Nook books to another device, like Kindle, Kobo, or Remarkable, you'll need to remove DRM from the Nook books first and then convert them to a compatible format for the new device.

##### Q: What's the difference between Nook and Kindle?

A: Amazon and Barnes & Noble have established their own ecosystems to provide e-books to their users. Kindle e-readers only support books purchased from Amazon Kindle and non-DRM Kindle-compatible formats, like AZW3 and MOBI. Nook e-readers only support books purchased from Barnes & Noble.

##### Q: Can I send Nook to kindle on Mac?

A: Sure, the method introuduced in this post also applies to Mac computer. You just need to download android emulator on mac, download nook books to mac, and then convert the nook to kindle format.
