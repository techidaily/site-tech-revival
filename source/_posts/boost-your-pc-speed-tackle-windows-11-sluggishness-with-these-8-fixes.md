## Stop Automatic Updates on Your Windows 11 PC with These Easy Tricks

Windows Update is a feature that automatically checks and provide updates for our computer.

 But sometimes it can be such a big pain that you want to turn it off.

 Here are 3 easy-to-follow methods you can try to**stop Windows update in Windows 10** .
