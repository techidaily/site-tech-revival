## Option 2: Directly Watch Movies on iPad with Netflix App 

![Netflix for iPad Watching](https://www.macxdvd.com/mac-dvd-video-converter-how-to/article-image/netflix-ipad.jpg) 

If you are a Netflix member, you can watch Netflix movies on iPad with app itself downloaded from the Apple App Store. As the [best movie app for iPad](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/), Netflix app enables you watch your favorite movies or TV shows on condition that you have a good network connection. And it's worth to mention that Netflix for iPad application only applies for iPad with iOS 8.0 or later. To watch watch movies with Netflix App for iPad, you can:

1.Power on your new iPad and connect the network

2.Download Netflix for iPad App and install it 

3.Log in Netflix account on iPad

4.Search the target movie/TV show and rent it

5.Watch the Netflix movie or TV show on your iPad

Undoubtedly, Netflix iOS app serves as the best solution to watch blockbusters on iPad if there is a good network connection. But [Netflix for iPad not working](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/)occurs now and then. So if you run into the situation, you can switch to option#1 to watch Netflix movies on iPad offline.
