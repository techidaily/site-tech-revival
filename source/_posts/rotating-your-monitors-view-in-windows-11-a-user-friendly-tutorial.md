## **Method 3: Change Group Policy Settings**

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/img_5b320153b5782.jpg)

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/img_5b32023756b85.jpg)
