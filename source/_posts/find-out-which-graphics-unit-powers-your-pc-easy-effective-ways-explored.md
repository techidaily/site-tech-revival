Usually, you can get the model name under category “Display adapters” in [Device Manager](https://tools.techidaily.com/drivereasy/download/) .  
  
 Refer following steps to download drivers from NVIDIA website.  
  
1\. Go to [NVIDIA download page](http://www.nvidia.com/Download/index.aspx)  .  
  
 2\. Select the product information and system information depending on the Graphics card and system version that you are using . (Here take “GeForce GEX 1080” and “Windows 7 64-bit” as example.) Then click on **SEARCH**  button and follow the on-screen instructions to download the driver.
