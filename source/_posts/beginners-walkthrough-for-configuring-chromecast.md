## How to arrange your desktop icons

 It takes you only a few steps to arrange the desktop icons. Here’s how to do it:

1) Right-click a blank area on your desktop to open the context menu.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/image-376.png)

 2) Click**Sort by** and choose one command (either Name, Size, Item type, or Date modified) from the right menu. These commands indicate how you want to arrange the icons.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/image-379.png)

 3) Now your desktop icons should be displayed in an orderly way. Besides, if you don’t like to arrange the icons manually, you may click**View > Auto arrange icons** and let Windows do the configuration for you.

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/image-381.png)

 These are the simple and useful steps that help you arrange the desktop icons. If you want to lock them in place, please move on to the next part.
