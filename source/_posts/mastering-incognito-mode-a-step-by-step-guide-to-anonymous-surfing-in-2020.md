## Quick Guide to Finding and Downloading Your HP Device's Windows 10 Drivers

To download HP drivers after you upgrade the system to Windows 10, you can go to [HP official website](http://www8.hp.com/sg/en/home.html) . Then download the driver you need from[Software & drivers section](http://support.hp.com/sg-en/drivers) .  
  
 Before you find and download drivers, you are required to get the device model name. For some products, you also need to know the specific operating system that your PC is running (32-bit or 64-bit).  
  
 Please refer to following steps to download driver for your HP product.  
  
 1\. In driver download page, enter your HP model number.(for instance, OfficeJet 2620)  
  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/04/img_57060ff1b9005.png)
  
 2\. Follow the on-screen instructions until you go to the driver download page. Then select the system as Windows 10\.
