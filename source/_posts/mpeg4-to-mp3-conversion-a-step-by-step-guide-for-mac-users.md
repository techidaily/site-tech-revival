## 3\. VLC Media Player

![Convert MPEG4 to MP3 on Mac VLC](https://www.macxdvd.com/mac-dvd-video-converter-how-to/article-image/vlc-extract-youtube.jpg) 

Convert MPEG4 to MP3 on Mac VLC

[VLC](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) is a feature-rich media player capable of free converting MPEGP4 MP4 file to MP3 on Mac. To separate MP3 audio from mpeg4 video on Mac OS: 

**Step 1**: Open up VLC if you already have installed the media player on Mac, head to Media > Convert/Save.

**Step 2**: Click "Add" button and select the MPEG4 media file you want to convert. 

**Step 3**: After the Convert window pops up, browse for a destination for the MP3 audio file. Label the MPEG4 file .mp3 and press Save button.

**Step 4**: Click on the Edit button, under the Audio Codec, choose MP3, you are allowed to adjust MP3 audio Bitrate, Sample rate as well, and click Save button. 

**Step 5**: Click "Start" button to start changing MPEG4 MP4 media file to MP3 on Mac. 

**The annoyance**: After all, VLC is a media player not a professional MPEG4 to MP3 converter for Mac. It fails to accept a wide range of media formats. Occasionally, sound quality may be terrible, this method is not recommended for users who are caring about the output audio quality and MPEG4 to MP3 conversion speed on Mac.
