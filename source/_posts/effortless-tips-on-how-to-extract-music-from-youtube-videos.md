## Stop Mouse Acceleration Easily - Tips & Tricks Inside

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/img_5b99da21b4121.jpg)

 If you want to get better accuracy when using tools and weapons in a game, you should try turning off the mouse acceleration on your PC.

## What is mouse acceleration?

**Mouse acceleration** , as its name implies, enables your mouse pointer to move faster and go a longer distance than you physically move your mouse.

 It’s convenient to do office work and web browsing with mouse acceleration, but it can also affect your aiming and movement accuracy in a game. So, gamers usually turn off mouse acceleration on computer to enhance gaming performance.
