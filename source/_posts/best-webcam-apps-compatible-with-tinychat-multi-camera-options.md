## ManyCam for tinychat

Search 

In Tinychat, open the "Camera options" window and select "ManyCam Virtual Webcam" or "ManyCam Video Source". Click on the "Finish" button.

![](https://manycam.com/build/images/help/settings/tinychat.jpg?v=751e3132d4)   
  
  
To choose ManyCam as the audio source, open the "Microphone options" window and select "ManyCam Virtual Microphone". Click on the "Continue" button.

![](https://manycam.com/build/images/help/settings/audio/tinychat.jpg?v=6a993a9a4d)
