## Top Jim Carrey Movies You Must See 

### Top 1: Ace Ventura: Pet Detective

This is an American comedy detective film in 1994 directed by Tom Shadyac and starring Jim Carrey. This movie mainly talks about a goofy detective who specializes in recovering lost animals and tries to find the kidnapped two weeks Miami Dolphins aquatic mascot named Snowflake. It grossed $107 million worldwide from a $15 million budget and the success of box office proved that it deserves the title of Jim Carrey best movie and [top 10 Hollywood movies](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/).

### Top 2: Silver Medal Winning Film - The Mask

Based on a series of comic books, The Mask is a 1994 American fantasy slapstick action comedy film directed by Chuck Russell. The Mask Jim Carrey tells a story about a mild-manned banker who finds an ancient mask one night that transforms him into a funny prankster with super power. This film was nominated for the [Academy Award](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/) for Best Visual Effects and Jim Carrey was nominated for a Golden Globe for his role in the meantime. 

### Top 3:- Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind 

Directed by Michel Gondry, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind is a 2004 romantic science-fiction comedy film. Jim Carrey stars as Joel Barish who was informed that his ex-girlfriend made her memories about their relationship deleted from her brain after done an experimental. In order not to be eliminated, Joel Barish decided to do the same procedure. By using the elements of science fiction, psychological thriller and a nonlinear narrative, it discusses the nature of memory and romantic love.

### Top 4:- Dumb and Dumber Ranks Fourth 

Starring Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels and directed by the Farrelly brothers, Dumb and Dumber is a 1994 American comedy film. It tells that two good friends are in their trip of returning a briefcase full of money to its owner and are pursued by a group of criminals. It is worth mentioning that its sequel named Dumb and Dumber To is scheduled to be released on November 14, 2014\. This film successfully achieved the 1995 MTV Movie Awards and was voted as the fifth greatest comedy film all time, the DVD movie was also on the list of top DVD rentals. 

### Top 5: The Truman Show Wins the 5th Place 

It is an American satirical comedy film directed by Peter Weir in 1998\. This is a story about a man who lives in a constructed reality television show and begins to discover the truth of his life. Some people believe that this movie with Jim Carrey is funny, tender and thought-provoking. Moreover, it received numerous nominations at the 71st Academy Awards, 56th Golden Globe Awards, 52nd British Academy Film Awards and The Saturn Awards.
