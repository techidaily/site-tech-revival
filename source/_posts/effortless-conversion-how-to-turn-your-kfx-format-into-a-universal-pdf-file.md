### 1\. What is KFX Format?

A KFX file is an eBook created in the Kindle Format 10, Amazon's successor to the AZW3 format. It encompasses an Amazon eBook, incorporating both text and metadata that describe the book. KFX files support enhanced typesetting capabilities that are not provided in previous formats, such as hyphenation, improved character placement and word spacing, and a more adaptable page layout and margins.

#### How to tell if a Kindle book is KFX format or not

If the Kindle book is described as Enhanced Typesetting enabled in its product details, then it is a KFX format book.

![books with enhanced typesettings](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/books-with-enhanced- typesettings.jpg)

The KFX eBook downloaded from the Amazon store depends on the device being used to download and read the eBook. It may be saved in the KFX format or in the AZW format. 

And KFX books downloaded with Kindle for PC/Mac is saved in **AZW format**. 

![kfx content Pc](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/kfx-content-Pc.png)

While it will be saved in KFX format if it is downloaded with Kindle E-ink Reader.

![kfx content device](http://www.epubor.com/images/uppic/kfx-content-device.png)
