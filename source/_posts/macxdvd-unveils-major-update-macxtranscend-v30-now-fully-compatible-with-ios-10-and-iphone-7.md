## Macxdvd Unveils Major Update: Macxtranscend v3.0 Now Fully Compatible with iOS 10 and iPhone 7

## MacX MediaTrans V3.0 Gets Same-Day Revolutionary Update along with iOS 10 Release 

Sep 18, 2016

![MacX MediaTrans updates](https://www.macxdvd.com/press-room/image/mt-upgrade.jpg) 

_MacXDVD announced overnight its same-day upgrade to MacX MediaTrans V3.0 to make it the most comprehensive data transfer for iOS 10\. This significant upgrade introduces a slew of all-new breakthrough features, extending the leading iPhone media transfer solution to advanced iPhone ringtone transferring & making and one-click management for iBook, VoiceMemo, iTunes U, Podcast and more. Now MacXDVD brings the most extensive and convenient data transfer between computer and iPhones/iPads/iPod running on all iOS versions- including iOS 10\._ 

Immediately following the arrival of iOS 10, MacXDVD Software, an acknowledged leading multimedia solution provider, today announced a major upgrade in [MacX MediaTrans V3.0](https://tools.techidaily.com/macxdvd/products/). MacX MediTrans users already have the easiest iPhone data transfer solution, now MacXDVD is adding support for five more contents: iPhone ringtone, iBook, VoiceMemo, iTunes U, Podcast. With the upgraded V3.0, the industry-leading iPhone manager will let users of iOS 10 or earlier versions manage & transfer data with maximum freedom, and make iDevices more agile, responsible and efficient. 

MacX MediaTrans V3.0 is fully compatible with iPhone 7/Plus and iOS 10 and is being updated constantly. Users of any iDevices can create and edit ringtone, and backup & transfer books, voice, music, videos, photos and more in one click with the latest version at: <https://www.macxdvd.com/mediatrans/>

"With the improved Continuity in iOS 10 and Sierra, Apple continues to emphasis on the interaction between iOS devices and Mac computers. While it transfers text, images or videos and uploads files to iCloud, we would like to expand this functionality even further," said Jack Han, CEO of MacXDVD, "In MacX MediaTrans V3.0, we have added industry-leading iPhone ringtone management and massive support for a range of new contents."

## What's new in MacX MediaTrans v3.0?

**Say Hello to world's advanced iPhone ringtone maker**  
The newly-landed iPhone ringtone management will sync songs from iPhone or computer as iPhone ringtone in one click: create iPhone ringtones by waveform cropping & editing and automatically convert music into iPhone ringtone files. 

**Manage music on iPhone iPad in just one click**  
The boosted music manager transfers any songs to iPhone, iPad and iPod, automatically converting music with incompatible audio formats into Apple supported MP3 and AAC losslessly. 

**Overall iPhone iPad audio management**   
Support now extends to iPhone ringtone, Voicememeo, iTunes U, Podcast to import, export, remove and auto convert those voices freely, making MacX MediaTrans V3.0 an overall solution to manage all audio on iDevices. 

**Full control over iPhone/iPad books**  
MacX MediaTrans V3.0 makes it simple to stay iBook library arranged: directly add or remove books in Epub, PDF and Audiobook; export books to computers and auto convert Epub into PDF, Html and TXT in one click. 

**Other highlights in MacX MediaTrans V3.0**  
1\. Totally fresh design.  
2\. Feedback log added to report any failure or error.

"MacXDVD keeps upgrading with iOS 10 updates to give users the most timely solutions. To achieve this, we have run hundreds of tests against the Beta versions," Jack continued, "With MacX MediaTrans V3.0, we believe we have executed the world' fastest and the most comprehensive data transfer solution to make the experience on iOS 10 or earlier even better."
